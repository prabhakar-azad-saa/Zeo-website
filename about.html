My Story

Zoe Dronfield is a passionate advocate for domestic abuse survivors, drawing from her own personal experience of surviving a near-fatal attack. Her journey from victim to survivor to thriver has shaped her mission to support others facing similar challenges.

With a background in business development and a natural talent for public speaking, Zoe combines her professional skills with her personal experience to create meaningful change in how society addresses domestic abuse.

She is the founder of several initiatives aimed at supporting survivors and raising awareness about domestic violence, working tirelessly to ensure that no one has to face abuse alone.
