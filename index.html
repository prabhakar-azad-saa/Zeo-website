About Zoe

Zoe Dronfield is a passionate advocate, speaker, and author dedicated to supporting survivors of domestic abuse. Her personal journey through coercive control and domestic violence has fueled her mission to help others break free from abusive relationships.

As a recognized expert in the field, Zoe works tirelessly to raise awareness about the signs of domestic abuse, provide practical tools for recovery, and advocate for policy changes that better protect survivors.

Through her writing, speaking engagements, and community initiatives, Zoe empowers individuals to reclaim their lives and build futures free from violence and fear.

Mind Over Manipulators

Mind Over Manipulators is the story of one woman’s journey through life, her relationships with abusive men and her fight to fix the broken justice systems in the UK.

After being tortured and almost murdered, Zoe was then dragged through family court to retain custody of her four-year-old daughter, who was placed in the care of a man who used this ‘opportunity’ to exact revenge. At the same time, Zoe was being intimidated from prison by her potential murderer and had to navigate both the criminal and family justice systems concurrently.

Whether you're currently in an abusive relationship, supporting someone who is, or rebuilding your life after abuse, this book provides the knowledge and empowerment needed to break free and thrive.
