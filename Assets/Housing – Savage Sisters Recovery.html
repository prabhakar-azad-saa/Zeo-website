A Whole-Health Program

We currently have three male and three female-houses located in South Philadelphia, all of which provide a safe and trauma-informed environment for our residents. Our program is dynamic and whole-health oriented, and provides added support to individuals who are suffering from substance abuse disorder.

We are S.I.R.

SIR stands for Savages in Recovery, and it is an initiative started to provide support to the men in our community. Savage is currently operating three men's houses in the Philadelphia region, which provides its residents with a safe and structured enviroment. Residents are also offered free resources including dual diagnosis trauma therapy, mindfulness workshops, kickboxing, apiary therapy and much more.
